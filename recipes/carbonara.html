Carbonara

A Classic Italian Dish

This classic Italian pasta dish is creamy, savory, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper. Serve immediately.
