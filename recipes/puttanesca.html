Spaghetti alla Puttanesca

This is a classic Italian pasta dish made with tomatoes, olives, capers, and anchovies.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 anchovy fillets, chopped
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 100g black olives, pitted
  • 2 tbsp capers
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and anchovies until fragrant.
  3. Add canned tomatoes, olives, capers, and chili flakes. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and toss it with the sauce.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.
